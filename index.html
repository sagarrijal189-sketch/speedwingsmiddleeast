Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the most common questions from employers and candidates.

How long does the recruitment process usually take?
+

For most positions, we complete the full recruitment cycle within 2‚Äì4 weeks, including shortlisting, interviews, selection, and documentation. Specialist or senior roles may take a little longer, while urgent and bulk-hiring projects can be fast-tracked based on your requirement.

What industries does SpeedWings Middle East support?
+

We work across multiple sectors including Hospitality & Tourism, Healthcare, Construction & Engineering, Retail, Logistics & Transport, Manufacturing, Admin & Support Services, IT & Technology, and Agriculture. Each industry is handled by recruiters who understand its specific standards and skill demands.

Do you handle visa and immigration processing?
+

Yes. We provide complete visa and immigration support, including document verification, visa application coordination, medicals, and travel arrangements. All procedures follow the official regulations of UAE, Saudi Arabia, Qatar, Oman, and other GCC countries.

How do your service fees work for employers?
+

Our fees depend on job category, hiring volume, origin country, and service scope. We follow a transparent pricing model with no hidden charges and can provide custom packages for long-term or bulk recruitment projects. Share your needs and we‚Äôll prepare a tailored proposal.

Do you charge any fees to candidates?
+

Our recruitment is aligned with ethical and legal guidelines of both origin and destination countries. Depending on the project and regulations, candidates may pay no recruitment fee or only minimal, clearly explained costs (such as medicals or documentation). We never apply hidden or surprise charges.

Do you provide support after the candidate joins?
+

Yes. We offer post-placement support for both employers and candidates, including onboarding guidance, follow-ups and, when agreed in advance, replacement options during the guarantee period. Our goal is to build long-term, successful partnerships.

Which countries do you recruit talent from?
+

We recruit from 25+ countries across South Asia, Southeast Asia, and Africa, including India, Nepal, Pakistan, Bangladesh, Sri Lanka, Philippines, Indonesia, Kenya, Uganda, Ghana and more. This gives our clients access to a wide, diverse, and highly skilled talent pool.
